Relaxing Spray

Relieves muscle tension and prepares your mind for deep sleep with our fast-absorbing spray, formulated with pure magnesium and natural lavender.

What is the Magnesium & Lavender Spray?

A relaxing spray with fast absorption, formulated with pure magnesium and 100% natural lavender essential oil. Designed to relieve muscle tension and prepare your body and mind for deep sleep, anytime, anywhere.

Why You'll Love It

  • Relieves muscle pains and cramps instantly.
  • Reduce anxiety and stress with lavender aroma.
  • Improves sleep quality when used before bed.
  • Natural formula, without harsh chemicals.
  • Amber glass bottle that protects the ingredients.

How to Use

Spray 4-6 times on tense areas (shoulders, neck, lower back) or on pulses like wrists and ankles for general relaxation. Use daily!

Pure Ingredients:

Distilled water, magnesium chloride, lavender essential oil, witch hazel (natural preservative).
